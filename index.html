[ESX] County Alive

County Alive is a ESX project to revamp the county and bring more players of your city to it! With many activities to do, this resource should improve the population of your county!

[ESX] Mechanic Tools

Mechanic tools gives players the ability to spawn tools (items) and move them to their liking for a more realistic, in-depth, role-play experience. The script currently has three tools: toolbox, engine hoist, and car lift.
At the time, the script is not job dependent, although I might make a change like this in the future. If you have any suggestions, please feel free to leave them in the comments!

[ESX] Body Loot

Body Loot allows players to loot dead NPC's for items set in the configuration file. To prevent from any unfair usage, the body (ped) is deleted from the world after being looted.

[ESX] Vending

Add vending machines to your city!

[ESX] New Number

Allow players to change their phone number! This was tested with GC_Phone!

[ESX] Police Buddy

Police Buddy is an ESX menu to allow players to obtain information about nearby vehicles and interact with NPC peds.

[ESX] Give License

A very simple script that gives players the ability to show their license to the closest player. Players are able to set their license status each time while giving it. Commands: /give-license [STATUS] /view-license [STATUS]

[ESX] Trash Search

Store and withdraw items from dumpsters around San Andreas!

[ESX] HUD Toggle

Easily toggle the ESX HUD on and off!

[ESX] Custom Storage

For each location in the config, you must add that society name to your database in the addon_inventory table. I have provided a base SQL file to make it easier

[ESX] Purchase Ammo

This is a script that allows players to purchase ammo for the weapons they currently have in their inventory at the default ESX ammu-nation locations.

[ESX] Enter Places

Easily add enterable places to your FiveM server!

[ESX] Sell To Players

This script allows you to sell items on your player to other players.
